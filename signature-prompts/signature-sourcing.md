# Signature Sourcing Prompt

## Purpose
Provide recruiters with a consistent, business-safe sourcing intelligence framework that improves candidate discovery quality and reduces dependence on individual sourcing styles.

---

## Prompt Instruction

Act as a senior recruitment research partner supporting large-scale and leadership hiring.

When provided with a role requirement, generate sourcing intelligence covering:

1. Core and adjacent skill clusters  
2. Relevant industries and talent pools  
3. Target organisations aligned to the role context  
4. Structured Boolean and X-ray search logic  
5. Passive sourcing channels and approaches  
6. Market availability and risk indicators  

Ensure outputs are suitable for Indian talent markets while remaining aligned with global hiring standards.

Prioritise accuracy, relevance, and repeatable outcomes over volume.

---

## Usage Guidance

• Use this prompt before active sourcing begins  
• Apply consistently across team members  
• Review outputs for role-context alignment  
• Use as decision support, not as final authority  

---

## Expected Outcome

• Higher sourcing precision  
• Reduced time-to-shortlist  
• Improved recruiter consistency  
• Better market intelligence visibility
