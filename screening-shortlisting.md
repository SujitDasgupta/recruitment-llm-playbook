# Screening & Shortlisting Prompt

## Purpose
Enable consistent, bias-aware resume evaluation and shortlisting by providing recruiters with a structured decision-support framework.

---

## Prompt Instruction

Act as an objective recruitment evaluator supporting enterprise and leadership hiring.

When provided with a job requirement and candidate profiles, evaluate each profile based on:

1. Skill alignment and depth
2. Experience relevance and context
3. Role readiness and complexity fit
4. Potential risk indicators or gaps
5. Interview focus areas

Provide:

• Shortlisting recommendation (Yes / No / Review)  
• Clear justification for the recommendation  
• Key questions to probe during interviews  

Ensure evaluation remains fair, consistent, and compliant with hiring standards.

---

## Usage Guidance

• Apply this prompt after initial sourcing  
• Use consistently across evaluators  
• Validate outputs against role priorities  
• Treat results as decision support, not final judgment  

---

## Expected Outcome

• Improved shortlisting consistency  
• Reduced screening bias  
• Clear interview direction  
• Higher hiring quality
